HYDERABAD: Chief Minister Y.S. Rajasekhara Reddy and Telugu Desam Party president N. Chandrababu Naidu on Monday hailed the success achieved by noted music director A.R. Rahman in winning two Oscars for his work in Slumdog Millionaire.

He also congratulated Rasool Pookutty for winning the Oscar for best sound mixing, Gulzar for the best song ‘Jai Ho’ and the film’s team for winning the best film award. Mr. Naidu, while expressing happiness, said Monday would be remembered as a glorious day for the Indian film industry.

Inspiration to all

Praja Rajyam president Chiranjeevi and general secretary Allu Arvind also congratulated Rahman and sound editor Pookutty for bagging the awards, and described it a ‘proud occasion’ for the country.
