 DMK Parliamentary Party leader T.R. Baalu on Monday met his party colleague and former Telecom Minister A. Raja in Tihar jail here.

Mr. Baalu enquired about the health of Mr. Raja, who is suffering from ulcer of the stomach and reflux problem.

Mr. Raja, arrested by CBI in connection with the scam in 2G spectrum allocation, is in Tihar jail after the court remanded him in judicial custody.
