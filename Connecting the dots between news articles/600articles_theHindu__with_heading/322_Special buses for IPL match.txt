 The Bangalore Metropolitan Transport Corporation will operate special buses from 4 p.m. onwards by extending its Big 10 service for cricket fans for the IPL fixture on Friday at Chinnaswamy Stadium.

While the service to the stadium will entail regular fares, passengers have to a pay a special fare of Rs. 20 for services after the match. All the routes will operate with series G-1 to G-12. Fans have been advised to shun private vehicles to avoid traffic jams.
