New Delhi: A CD purportedly showing key witness in the Nitish Katara murder case, Ajay Katara, admitting to taking bribe for testifying in the case was on Saturday played in a Delhi court. After watching the CD for about 15 to 20 minutes, the court reserved its order on the application filed under Section 311 of the CrPC dealing with court’s power to recall witnesses, saying that it would be pronounced later in the day.
