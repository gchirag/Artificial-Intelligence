TDP supporters and leaders celebrated the victory of Anna Hazare here on Sunday by distributing sweets, playing music and raising slogans. Enthusiastic activists also burst crackers.

Addressing the gathering TDP district president Somisetty Venkateswarlu and party leader V. Rambhupal Choudary said Anna Hazare episode was the great victory for the people of the country who could bring pressure on the the political leadership to concede the demand. Describing Anna Hazare as the second Gandhi, the leaders said the whole country was fed up with the corruption and wanted to dump the corrupt officials and leaders. Anna Hazare ignited people's passion for corruption-free society and added that government should have conceded the demand long ago considering the public mood. They said the TDP would actively take part in any movement against corruption in future too.
