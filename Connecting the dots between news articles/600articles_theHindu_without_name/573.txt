 The Andhra Pradesh Kaulu Rytula Sangham on Friday expressed serious concern over a spate of suicides by sharecroppers in West Godavari district in the recent past. According to B. Balaram, spokesman of the sangham, said as many as 10 tenant farmers had committed suicide in the last five days following extensive crop damage due to rains and floods.

He wanted the State government to offer a package to end the trend of suicides among the sharecroppers.
