Speakers at a workshop on ‘Agrarian distress and farmers suicides in Andhra Pradesh' wanted the academics and research scholars to find reasons for increased suicides of farmers in the State. At the workshop sponsored by the UGC under a major research project here at Pingali Government College for Women, N.G. Ranga university Registrar L. Jalapathi Rao said declining land holdings, reduced water availability and exploitation by middlemen were driving farmers to take the extreme step.
