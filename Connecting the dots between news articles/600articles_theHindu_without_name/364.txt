AHMEDABAD: Siddiqui Ibrahim Hatila, a prime accused in the Godhra train carnage, was arrested from his residence in Godhra on Monday morning.

Acting on a tip-off, the police kept a watch on his Mohammadi Mohalla residence in the town.

The police believed that Hatila, absconding since the carnage on February 27, 2002, which triggered communal riots, was one of those plotted the fire in the S-6 coach of the Sabarmati Express in which 59 “kar sevaks” were burnt alive.
