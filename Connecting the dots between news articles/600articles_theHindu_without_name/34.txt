 Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav on Monday announced Rs. 20 lakh in financial assistance for the family of the Delhi gang-rape victim, who hailed from Ballia district.

Mr. Yadav had earlier announced a job for a family member and Rs. 5 lakh in financial assistance.
